Tips

  1. Acquire more points at higher difficulty levels and for peeling smaller good guys.
  2. Bounce-Back! When you are against a blue wall, click the arrow pointing in the direction of the wall to go the opposite direction.
  3. Crossover! When you are next to a corner ↬ ↫ ↬ ↫ This will bump you diagonally. When you are next to one, click the arrow pointing toward it.
  4. Try combining a Crossover with a Bounce-Back!
